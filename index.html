What changed from Bala's v1

Bala's original plan trained straight from Porto to Vigo, then walked Vigo → Santiago (not truly coastal, as he flagged himself). This draft keeps that Vigo → Santiago finish — it's also what qualifies for the Compostela certificate — and adds 4 coastal walking days between Porto and Vigo: the boardwalks past Póvoa, the Apúlia stilt granaries, and the two highest-rated stretches on the whole route — the Galicia border crossing through A Guarda & Oia's monastery, and the lighthouse walk into Baiona. Least-scenic connecting stretches are taxied. 10 walking days total, ~16.5km/day average.

Day by day

  • Day 1 · Sun 27 Sep Traveltravel
    Depart Chennai / Bay Area → arrive Lisbon. Check in, relax.
  • Day 2 · Mon 28 Sep Lisbon / Sintra
    Sintra day trip — lunch at Arun's home, dinner in Cascais or Sintra.
  • Day 3 · Tue 29 Sep Porto
    Train Lisbon → Porto (~3h). Ribeira, Douro, Old Town.
  • Day 4 · Wed 30 Sep Póvoa de Varzimwalk · 14km
    Taxi Porto → Labruge, walk Labruge → Póvoa de Varzim — coastal boardwalks.
  • Day 5 · Thu 1 Oct Viana do Castelowalk · 16km
    Taxi Póvoa → Aguçadoura, walk to Marinhas via the Apúlia stilt granaries, taxi on to Viana do Castelo.
  • Day 6 · Fri 2 Oct Porto Mougás, Spainwalk · 23.5km
    Taxi Viana → Caminha, walk into Galicia via A Guarda and Oia's monastery. Highest-rated scenic stage on the route.
  • Day 7 · Sat 3 Oct Vigowalk · 16km
    Walk Porto Mougás → Baiona (Cabo Silleiro lighthouse), taxi Baiona → Vigo.
  • Day 8 · Sun 4 Oct Redondelawalk · 15.4km
    Vigo → Redondela. Coastal/forest — Compostela "last 100km" starts here.
  • Day 9 · Mon 5 Oct Pontevedrawalk · 19.8km
    Redondela → Pontevedra. One of the prettiest stages on the whole Camino.
  • Day 10 · Tue 6 Oct Caldas de Reiswalk · 21.1km
    Pontevedra → Caldas de Reis.
  • Day 11 · Wed 7 Oct Padrónwalk · 18.6km
    Caldas de Reis → Padrón. Historic pilgrim town.
  • Day 12 · Thu 8 Oct Padrónbuffer
    Rest / weather / injury contingency day.
  • Day 13 · Fri 9 Oct Santiagowalk · 24.4km
    Padrón → Santiago, walked in full to keep everyone eligible for the Compostela. Cathedral, Pilgrim Mass, celebration dinner.
  • Day 14 · Sat 10 Oct Traveltravel
    Fly Santiago → Madrid → home.
  • Day 15 · Sun 11 Oct Hometravel
    Arrival.
